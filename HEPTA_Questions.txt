QUESTION 1: Gesture Taxonomy & Classification
------------------------------------------------
【选择题】Which category describes a gesture where the user draws a "?" symbol to invoke help?
A. Physical gesture
B. Metaphorical gesture  
C. Symbolic gesture
D. Abstract gesture

【简答题】(≤150 words)
Explain the difference between Object-Centric and World-Dependent binding in the Taxonomy of Surface Gestures. Provide one concrete example for each to illustrate how the same physical gesture (e.g., dragging) would function differently under these two binding types.

QUESTION 2: User-Defined Gesture Methodology
------------------------------------------------
【选择题】In a Guessability Study, what is the primary purpose of showing participants the "referent" (effect) before asking for the "sign" (gesture)?
A. To teach participants the correct gesture
B. To avoid designer bias and elicit natural mental models
C. To demonstrate the technical capabilities of the system
D. To ensure participants use two hands instead of one

【简答题】(≤150 words)
Describe the formula for calculating Agreement Score (A) in user-defined gesture research. Explain why a low agreement score for a specific command suggests the need for on-screen widgets rather than gestures.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

QUESTION 3: Gesture Design Principles
------------------------------------------------
【选择题】According to the study, which statement about user finger usage is TRUE?
A. Users prefer using exactly 3 fingers for all zooming operations
B. Finger count matters significantly for distinguishing command types
C. Users treat 1-3 fingers as a "single point" and vary usage casually
D. Five-finger gestures are universally preferred for complex operations

【简答题】(≤150 words)
